So Who Is This Guy!?

He is a seasoned educator and communicator that specializes in community outreach and development of communication with families and schools. He focuses on developing relationships to build a strong work ethic in children and adults. He has the ability to manage multiple complex projects and goals at once. He excels at designing curriculum to guide long term growth for a project or program.


He recently began attending the Austin Coding Academy and began his path into web design. He is in the process of learning HTML, CSS, and javascript through the class. In his free time, he is investigating other coding languages to make himself as well versed as he can.







Has He Done Anything!?

Eric is a regular performer on euphonium, trombone, and tuba. He has performed with The Austin Cinematic Symhpony, Austin Civic Orchestra, Cedar Park Winds, Austin Brass Band, Bat City Brass, Best Best Best Friends Brass Band, Steady Legend (ska here in Austin), and many other groups.

Eric is a music educator and a budding composer and coder. Below are a few links of pieces he's written and websites he's designed.
