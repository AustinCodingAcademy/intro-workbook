Welcome to Towers of Hanoi

Help relocate the magical blocks from Tower One to Tower Three! Your mission is to place the blocks in Tower Three just as they appear in Tower One, with the smallest on top and largest on bottom. You may only move one block at a time, starting with the topmost block, and you may never place a larger block on a smaller block. The timer will begin when you move your first block. How fast can you complete this mission?
