About the Barkeeps

The Bark and Bar began as an idea in 2016 and sprang forth from Kat and Wes' mutual love of dogs, wine, and food.

It was important to us that we provide a place for dogs to interact and socialize with each other. In doing so, we also wanted to offer a place for the parents to mingle, to imbibe on local creations of drink and food.

We feel that The Bark and Bar has accomplished this. We were welcomed by the community and have welcomed dogs, and their families, with the same warmth. We hope you enjoy your time with us. Feel free to say "Hi!" to Bastian, Bonks, and Natty. They love the attention!
