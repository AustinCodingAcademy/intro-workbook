a little about me.

Lisa Umhey

Web Developer & Florist

Hello, I'm Lisa. I am a student at Austin Coding Academy, where I have been learning HTML, CSS and Javascript. I am in the beginning stages of learning the skills it takes to become a full-stack developer. I recently became interested this career path after realizing I wasn't fully satisfied with the way I use my brain in my current work. As a florist, I have the opportunity to be creative, meticulous, knowledgable, and collaborative to make beautiful things. And I find so much satisfaction in this. My imagination loves this work. But I also feel like something's missing. You see, at my core, in addition to being highly imaginative, I am a learner and problem-solver. And I wish I had more opportunity to leverage these strengths on a daily basis, without giving up the things I love about my current work. After conversations with a developer friend, I decided to give coding a try via codecademy.com. After working through the tutorials for several languages, I knew it was a strong fit. I decided to find a professional program to help me make the shift from florist to developer, and here I am. Once I graduate in December 2018, my goal is to attain a position as a junior developer and continue my journey into the tech field. In the long-run, I am very inspired by the potential of AI and dream of using this technology to help improve people's lives. In the meantime, I would love to intern with your team or volunteer on your next passion project. Let's collaborate!
