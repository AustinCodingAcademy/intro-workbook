Austin Cats and Dogs Rescue

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue and re-home cats and dogs to a forever home, not just any home.

We also want to educate and build a community that cares for, helps and protects all cats and dogs.

Many of these animals are abused, neglected, or on the streets because their owner moved and left them. Some animals need a new home because their owner can no longer care for them.

We are always in need of items from our wish list.
