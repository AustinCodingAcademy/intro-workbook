About

From Brewer to Front End Web Developer.

  • 2014-2015

    Humble Beginnings

    I started out as an Assistant Brewer in North Carolina. I followed my passion & I got to do great things in the beer community. I loved the science of brewing, but what really intrigued me was the technology behind the process.

  • 2015-2016

    Love Affair with Technology

    After moving to Austin, I started to work for The University of Texas at Austin as a Desktop Support Specialist. I worked with Faculty & Staff to not only solve technical issues but to deliver great customer support. Here is where I learned more about Web Development, which created a fire insdie me to learn everything I can about the Industy.

  • MAY 2016

    Transition to Education

    I started classes at Austin Coding Academy. This is a 10-week bootcamp for Front End Web Development. I mastered my skills in HTML5 & CSS3, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, Wireframing and other metheodologies of Front End Web Development.

  • July 2016

    A New Phase

    The combination of Web Development Skills, Passion & Customer Service, allows me to provide an unparalleled experince for my client. This is the foundation of my success in Web Development.
