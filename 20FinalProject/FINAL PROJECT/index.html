Who I am

My name is Madeline Logan but please, call me Maddie! I am a lover of music, survivor of code and an expert dabbler in service. When I'm not doing those things you can find me either on the bike trail around the lake, seeing a show downtown, or at karaoke night belting out Erykah Badu.

Where I've been

Orignally from Dallas, I attended the University of Arkansas in Fayetteville from 2009 to 2013. There I studied Communications and was a photographer for the school newspaper, The Traveler, also working as a radio DJ at the student-run radio station KXUA 88.3. I worked as a server through most of college at a small mom-n-pop pizza joint. After college I felt my life was becoming monotonous so I became wanderlust and decided to travel to Chile. Those two weeks in Chile were the catalyst for me to change my life and move to back to Texas. I've been in Austin for two years working in casual fine-dining and recently attended Austin Coding Academy and currently pursuing a career in web development.

What I do

I'm currently enrolled in the Intermediate course at Austin Coding Academy learning JavaScript and hating the process but loving the results.
