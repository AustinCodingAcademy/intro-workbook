Irreplicable Illustration Equals Memorable Content

Hi, my name is Zeke Guadiana.

Whether it's a fully realized consumer product, an illustration, a website, an animation, a game sprite, or a simple sketch, I love to draw on many different styles to accomplish illustrations and designs. These styles come from both art you provide me as an example and from the values you want to convey within the piece.

In the process of designing something together we can discuss your goals, your audiences expectations, the overall look and feel, many other facets to create something completely unique. The process is as involved as you'd like it to be.
