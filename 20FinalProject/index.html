Process

I provide you with a road map for working with me.

  • Consultation

    I get to know you.

    I learn what's important to you, your business and your vision. The best results come when I understand your needs, and you understand my process.

  • Development

    We design the basic outline of your site and provide you with weekly updates.

    We create the foundation of what will be your site. During this time, content, layout, and functionality may change several times.

  • Testing

    Your site nears completion and we begin an intensive testing phase.

    A great site fails to be great if it does not work. When your site launches, it has been rigorously tested with the most popular platforms to ensure cross functionial compatibility and security.

  • Final Revisions

    Our final meeting before launch

    Last second revisions, concerns, and updates are made here. We make sure your site expresses your vision and is easy to undertstand and navigate. Your clients will have an optimal experience.

  • Launch

    Your site is live!

    Once your site launches, you will be able to access it from your custom domain and begin directing current and future clients to your landing page.
