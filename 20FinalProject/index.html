a little bit about me

I've been in the software industry for close to 10 years and recently made the jump into the development side of things. It's been fascinating (and sometimes frustrating) trying to solve puzzles with code. Thanks to the killer crew of staff at Austin Coding Academy, I've come a lot farther than I imagined.

With experience in product marketing, product management and now development, I have a solid understanding of the end-to-end software development process - from planning to code to go-to-market.
