HOW DOES IT WORK?

Fungi Buddies

Plants have natural relationships with local fungi species that help them ward off diseases, partition nutrients, and increase the reach of their roots.

Savvy Sowing

We use these natural relationships to tailor crop care with local fungi to increase crop yields and grow heartier, healthier food.

Grow Big

The nutrients transported through the soil by these fungi increase bioavailability of nutrients and microdiversity for far past the single crop cycle-improving the quality of soil in compromised natural environments.

MUSHROOMS ARE CHANGING THE WORLD

Renowned mycologist Paul Stamets discusses some of the ways mushrooms are reversing climate change and ecological devastation, from absorbing oil and purifying water poisoned by factory farms to new building material, new medicine, and new pesticides.
