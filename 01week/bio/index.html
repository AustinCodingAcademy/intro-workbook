About Me

I grew up in the (not so sparkling) city by the sea of Corpus Christi, TX. After high school, I attended Texas State University in San Marcos to pursue a BFA in Communication Design and naturally migrated to Austin after graduation.

Shortly after relocation, I landed a job as a design intern at a one-man graphic design studio where I've worked ever since. I'm currently a full-time employee and we're still (mostly) a two person team.
