About Me

Born in Mexico and raised in Amarillo, I moved to Austin about three years ago. During the day I teach 4th graders a thing or two about math and science. This is the first year I've taught 4th grade and my previous years of teaching have all been in 3rd grade.

In my early years I played football in middle school and soccer in high school. During college I played in an indoor soccer team. Most tiring sport ever! My first job ever was with my uncle working out of his Mexican food truck back home in Amarillo. Best perk: tacos all day.

During my freetime, I like to go hiking and swimming. I enjoy travelling but don't get to travel often. I also enjoy going to concerts and watching local bands perform at small venues.

Why I am Learning to Code

I am learning to code because technology and programming are the future. From job automation to Alexa turning your lights off at home, its all accomplished because of code. While I enjoy teaching, unless I want to move to administration, there isn't anywhere to move up on the ladder. With programming, I would hope to be able to develop apps or programs that could be used for educational purposes.
