Just A Small Town Girl


Maggie Rosenbusch lives in Round Rock, Texas with her sister, 2 dogs and cat. She has spent the last 5 years working for her father's company RemoteCOM. The company works with probation and parole agencies around the United States to remotely monitor computers, smart-phones and tablets of offenders. Maggie has worked through multiple poisitions in the company, to create a well rounded understanding of how it functions. Her current position is data monitoring and providing guidance to the technical support staff.
