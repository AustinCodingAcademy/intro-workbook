About Me:

I am a 26 year old University of Texas at Austin graduate. I currently work as a contractor for Facebook where my daily tasks include working with Ad Account
Managers to determine if their clients' ads meet Facebook policy standards. I also look for trends in advertising to determine ad policy.

In my off time I am spending most of my time building my skills as a web developer. When I feel that my skills are adequate, I will move on to learning more difficult
code. I also spend my time practicing guitar, jamming with my friends, or finding concerts to attend. When I am not trying to improve my music and coding skills,
I am usually trying to up my skills playing Uncharted 4 on Playstation 4.

Why I am Learning to Code:
Like most people, I believe coding is the future. Every company that wants to be relevant has a web page. These web pages need to be built and maintained to stay
relevant. The job market for web development will most likey continue to grow for some time. This is where I want to begin. From that point, I want to continue
developing my coding skills so that one day I can create video games. It is something that has interested me since I was young. To sum up, I believe coders will provide
a service to all sorts of business while leading the world in technoligical development for time to come.
