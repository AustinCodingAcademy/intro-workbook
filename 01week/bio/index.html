About Me:

Why I am Learning How to Code:

I've always enjoyed building things and taking things apart. I considered learning programming in high school, but felt there wasn't a culture match so-to-speak. I thought I'd be meeting people like from the film "Hackers", a film I idolized because of the portrayal of rebellious, independently-minded hackers. Instead my high school computer class was just a bunch of uptight, conservative nerd bro guys. When I grew older I realized you're never really going to be happy in a work environment unless you set your mind to being happy, so I got over that and decided to learn anyway. I'm here now because I've tried to learn on my own, but always struggled to finish courses once they got harder. I am looking for mentorship.
