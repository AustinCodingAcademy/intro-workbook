ALPHA DOG

Services

Dog Walking

ALPHA DOG provides peace of mind for you when your busy schedule does not allow the time your dogs deserve.
We are available to walk your dog Monday – Friday or just on the days your schedule is most jammed. We can customize a schedule that best meets Rover’s needs. We offer daily walking visits of 30 per walk.

Dog Sitting

Dog sitting is the perfect choice for for dogs who can safely be allowed to roam the house in your absence. A member of the ALPHA DOG team visits with your dog at least three times per day.

Pet Transport

Once again that busy schedule of yours makes it difficult for you to take your pet to the vet or for his or her grooming appointment.
We provide the peace of mind that comes with the knowledge that your dog will be lovingly and safety transported to the vet or groomer. Leave the driving to us!
