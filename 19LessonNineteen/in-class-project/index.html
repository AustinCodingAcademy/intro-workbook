who is haley

In high school, my science teacher always started the year off by telling the class "the more you know, the more you know you don't know." It has become the passion behind what I do ever since. It is important to continue to learn and growth daily through the knowledge you have already gained and continue to gain.

I left high school to be a doctor... here I am a designer. I know-odd transition, but my passion for science and the way things work is still strong. I enjoy finding solutions to problems, creating visual pathways and content, and building foundations of work that relate to each other.

I believe animals are the true cure for sadness, crocheting is the new cool thing to do, photography relaxes the soul, and there is never an excuse not to travel.
