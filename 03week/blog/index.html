Grand Canyon

This was my first visit to the Grand Canyon and it did not disappoint. I was on a 3 week road trip with my girlfriend at the time, now Fiance. We were lucky enough to find a primitive campsite right outside of the Grand Canyon in the Kaibab National Forest. I was shocked that it was free. Granted there were no bathrooms or showers but we had plenty of room to pitch a tent, put up our hammocks, and make a fire pit without being disturbed by any nearby neighbors like the over crowded campgrounds inside the Grand Canyon.
