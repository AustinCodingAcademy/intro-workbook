Why the Sausage is King

The sausages is king because all lesser meats must bow down before it! Only the sausage has the flavor of many mysterious animals, rather than just one measly species!

Fancy steaks have fancy marbeling. And it will cost you $12 per lb at the HEB. Sausage, on the other hand, has perfectly equal distribution of the fat, at a price of about $2-$3 per lb.

To smoke a brisket, you must smoke for 10-16 hours. To smoke ribs, you must smoke for 3-6 hours. But sausage, that will only take 1-3 hours MAX!
