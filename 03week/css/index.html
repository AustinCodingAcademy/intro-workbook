Trump Ban Affecting Scientific Collaborations

The signed executive order will deny Syrian refugees entry to the US,

suspend all refugee admissions for 120 days, and block citizens of seven

countries from entering the US for at least 90 days. Those affected by

the travel ban include scientists, some of whom are speaking out about

how the order will affect their work and broader scientific community.
