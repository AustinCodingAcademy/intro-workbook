Austin Coding Academy offers part-time coding programs for professionals either for new careers or career improvement. Rather than some full-time coding classes, ACA offers classes two times a week with weekend study programs. Tuition is also cheaper than other coding schools.

ACA has been operating in Austin for several years, and recently launched in the San Antonio market, said co-founder Chris Sica. For the Houston launch, ACA plans to focus on connecting with the city's big industries: energy and health care.
