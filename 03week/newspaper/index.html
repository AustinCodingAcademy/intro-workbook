Local Man Finds Success in Web Development

Eric Nogelmeier, a Eunice local, has defied the odds and escaped his oilfield roots.Although making websites since a teenager he re-realized his dream of one day becoming a web developer, years after completing college.

Starting at Austin Coding Academy in January of 2017, he spent 5 days a week studying and building webpages, struggling week after week to learn the basics and turn it into a viable career. After learning the basics, he started to network, perform in hackathons, freelanced websites and volunteered to work in other groups to gain experience

Eric now enjoys what he does, no longer having to smell like gasoline and oil everyday. In fact he loves his work and his life.

If you'd like to know more about Eric and his exciting life check out his Spain Blog.
