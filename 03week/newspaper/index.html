Local Becomes Web Developer

Mariana Perez, a now Web-developer for the famous company AirBandB, is bringing her success to share with the city that raised her.

She was born in 1991 to Ricardo and Maria Hortencia. Mariana was always ahead of her time. She graduated early at the age of 16 then went off to obtain her Bachelors in fine arts only to become a tech guru.

Mariana says that she plans on brining her knowledge to Eagle Pass by starting a coding bootcamp.

It would be wonderful to one day be a city that no one ever thought would be technologically savy.

The future is here.
