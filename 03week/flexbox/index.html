Breaking News!


Today Greg Swan announced that he will be leaving the restaurant industry. In a statement he made on Monday, he said he wishes to leave behind a life of waiting tables and hop over to a different side of the tracks. what does he intend to do you might ask? WEB DEVELOPMENT.

He claims that he has had always had an eye for design, and with the ability to combine creativity and logic, there isn’t a better fit for his diverse array of talents. Greg began studying web development back in September of last year and instantly fell in love with it. Since beginning his academic journey he has been learning HTML and CSS- the cornerstones of any great website. He is currently enrolled in Austin Coding Academy’s intro course and intends to finish all three classes by September.

By the end of his education Greg hopes to be fluent in a variety of programming languages and will be heading out on his own in search of a fantastic . company to work for.

Keep your eyes peeled start-ups, Greg is building steam and will be available to begin working this fall.
