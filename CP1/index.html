About Rosita's Al Pastor

"Tacos al pastor – housemade flour tortillas stuffed with meats carved off a spit grill – are the specialty at this no-frills, counter-serve East Riverside spot. A permanent food trailer in the parking lot keeps the menu alive into the wee hours and is known for drawing clubgoing crowds."
- Zagat
