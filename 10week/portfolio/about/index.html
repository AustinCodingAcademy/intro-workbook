About

Welcome to my site! Here you will find samples of my work and learn a little about me and what areas of web development I focus on. I am originally from San Jose, California, and I now live in Austin, Texas, with my wife and our cat.


I am an avid crew rower with the Austin Rowing Club, I enjoy reading and going to the movies and I love tech. I got bit by the data bug a few years ago and I have loved datasets, data architecture and information science ever since. (Perhaps it comes from my youth and my love of libraries!)


I have formerly worked for Accenture at Google in business operations for the Chrome division. I oversaw the international shipments of Chrome OS laptops and Chromechast devices for Google marketing events. I also have worked for Apple and Comcast where I created inventory managing systems for both companies retail assets.
