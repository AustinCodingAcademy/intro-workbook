Salvador Rodriguez Artwork

My work is the result of a detailed observational study of nature, which can be seen in the composition and balance of loud and soft colors, producing pleasure and tranquility to contemplate.

Biography

Born in Tijuana, Baja California Norte, on 1968, from an early age he discovered his skills in drawing and painting, he liked to go outside the city to climb the hills and spend long periods appreciating and memorizing every detail of landscape and then put them into drawings or paintings. It was in high school when he demonstrated his skills in painting. He participated and won first place at the local and national level, which won him a scholarship in the workshop at Universidad Autónoma de Baja California to study painting with Master Francisco Chavez Corrujedo and where he was part of several group exhibitions. His work is the result of a detailed observational study of nature, which can be seen in the composition and balance of loud and soft colors, producing pleasure and tranquility to contemplate. Currently residing in Austin, Texas.
