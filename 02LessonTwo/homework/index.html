Bio

As I learn and share more about Web Development, I am coming across many people who do Web Development and Design. I recently had a conversation with an acquaintance where he asked me some questions to help me find my place in the world of Web Development.

One question he asked was "Would you consider yourself as a creative type person or more mathematical?" I have always struggled with this question, because I believe that there is something more than just 1 or the other. My reply, "I consider myself to be both creative and mathematical. That's a hard question for me to answer, my background is both, and I am happiest when those worlds collide." I am happiest when I use my brain in a creative way, coming up with a solution is not always a mathematical process. Some of the best solutions are creative. I believe this takes a person who is both to their core. I want to create Web Applications not because I am creative, or because I am mathematical but because I am a perfect storm of both.
