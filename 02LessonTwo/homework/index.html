About me

Hi. My name is Presley and I am 23 years old. I grew up in Rowlett, TX and moved to Austin five years ago to attend the University of Texas. I graduated in December of 2015 with a Rhetoric and Writing degree and a minor in Women and Gender Studies. In my spare time, I enjoy going to bars, watching movies, sitting around campfires, writing stories, and drunk dancing.
