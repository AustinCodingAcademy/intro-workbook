Alex Gaw

bio

Alex Gaw is a budding web developer who has a keen interest in...pretty much everything. He currently works full time as a paralegal in downtown Austin, but he hopes to find a job as a developer in the not-too-distant future. In addition to being a paralegal, Alex teaches swing dancing with his wife Samantha at various points in Austin and, occasionally, around Texas. When he's not doing that, he's making and drinking classic cocktails, woodworking, lifting upsettingly heavy—to him—weights, or gazing lovingly at his cat.
