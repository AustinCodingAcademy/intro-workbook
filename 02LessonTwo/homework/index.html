Biography

I grew up a military brat. My dad was an officer in the U.S. Air Force. I had to move around to many different places. I had to say good-bye to many friends, but, luckily made new ones fast. As a kid, I didn't appreciate how fortunate I was to live in all these places. Many people don't even have a chance to leave their hometown. I also think it has helped me to become a better person cause I'm good at change and adapting to new situations. I also can handle a wide range of personalitites. It's amazing how the different people can be depending on what region of the country they are from. I'm glad I've had the opportunity to live in such a variety of places.
