About

I am a musician and web developer currently residing in Austin, Texas.
I am an experienced electric and acoustic bassist, and played and recorded with well established artists throughout the South Eastern United States from 2005-2010.

As an improviser, composer, and electronic music enthusiast, I use modern recording software like Ableton Live! to capture sequential chord progressions on piano and guitar and add drum parts in MIDI.

When a composition is given form, I utlize my self created backing tracks as a solid canvas to paint improvised musical ideas on bass guitar. (See Video Below).
