Health Journey

I am also in the middle of what I hope is a 100 pound weight loss journey that started April 2015.
I have lost almost 70 pounds and have accomplished something I once thought was impossible.
A little over a year ago I was sedentary, broke, hopeless and felt so uncomfortable physically that I didn't even want to leave my house.
A little over a year later I am active, broke, hopeful ,and feel strong, confident and grounded.
