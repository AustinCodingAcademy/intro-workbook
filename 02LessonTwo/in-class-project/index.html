The Hideout

Who We Are

The Hideout is downtown Austin’s oldest independent coffee house. We are open morning to night, weekdays and weekends and serve beer, wine, fresh sandwiches, pastries, ice cream, as well as an assortment of beverages and coffee drinks.

We proudly feature vegan ice cream from our friends at Sweet Ritual and baked goods from Easy Tiger, and our specialty craft cocktails were designed by Laura Nixon of Bad Dog Barcraft.
