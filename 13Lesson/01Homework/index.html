Contract Brand Manager

Job description:

Best of Billy is looking for a contractor to help us maintain our brand on all platforms. This person will be resposible for small changes to the website such as updating information about the band's whereabouts, social media postings, marketing campaigns to fans, and much more.

Contractor is expected to:

  • Make changes to website as needed, including design and content.
  • Manage all social media and forums (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, Bandcamp)
  • Maintain database of newsletter emails
  • Create and manage unique and engaging email campaigns for fans such as ticket giveaways to upcoming shows, cover contests, etc

Requirements:

  • Must have 1 year of brand management related experience
  • Must be master of web design (HTML5, CSS3, bootstrap, responsive design, etc)
  • Must be a wordsmith in all forms from 140 character tweets to long form emails
  • Must be able to travel and work nights in order to capture pictures and content from live shows
