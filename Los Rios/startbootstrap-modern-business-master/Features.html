Three Sisters Lodging Located In Beautiful Camp Wood, Texas – Come By & Check Us Out!

Three Sisters Scenic Lodging: Our cabins are nestled in the hills of the beautiful Texas Hill Country along the Nueces river. We have views of the beautiful Hill Country all around us and the crystal clear waters of the Nueces river.

Short Drive from Two Different National Parks!

We are just a short distance from Garner State Park, Lost Maples State Natural Area, and the Devils Sink Hole State Natural Area.

Assortment Of Texas Wildlife from Deer, Turkey, Auodad and more…

The Texas Hill Country offers a great variety of wildlife. We have Whitetail Deer, Axis Deer, wild hogs, Black Buck Antelope, and much, much more! It is a hunters paradise.

We Can Host Family Reunions, Group Get Together, Business Retreats & More..

We offer a covered pavilion with electric and full kitchen. The kitchen includes electric stove with oven, micro wave, full sized refrigerator, and running water. We also have horse shoe and washer pits.

Pet Friendly

We are pet friendly here at Three Sisters Lodging and Event Center. All we ask is that you pick up after your pets and keep all dogs on a leash. We have a fenced in dog run with water. And we do require a 10.00 dollar pet fee.
