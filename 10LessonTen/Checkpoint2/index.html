About Cow Tipping Creamery

We love ice cream! Every town, every city, every country we go to, we search out local ice cream shops, talk to the artists, sample their creations and learn something new. When we decided to endevour on our own ice cream adventure, we knew we wanted to do something very different from all the other creameries in our town. Since there were already quite a few hard scoop ice cream shops in Austin, we decided to make our mark with unique soft serve creations. People have an emotional attachment to soft serve, childhood memories. Soft serve has not really evolved since we were kids and we wanted to change the way people think about it and let them experience it in a whole different way. It was time for soft serve to evolve and we felt like we were up to the challenge of making it happen!
