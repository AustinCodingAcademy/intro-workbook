How to Avoid the Clash of Marriage & Career

Hacking UI is a blog that ACA turned me onto. There are great articles, both technical in nature as well as more holisitic and inspirational in delivery. This one article is of particular importance to me as I am a new father at the starting line of a career that will prove challenging and full of sacrifice. The Most Exclusive Restaurant in America

This a story I recently found and loved. I believe the main point of the article is that as consumers we believe what we want about something. The assumption that this is the most exclusive restaurant in America is subjective. The hype is contrived and then scaled up to its enormous proportions by the media outlets and how they choose to write about it. In reality this is a very talented man that picks a few people to serve a week and keeps everything the restaurant does and is under tight secret.
