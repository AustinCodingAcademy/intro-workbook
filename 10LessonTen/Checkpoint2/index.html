Traveling Is Good For The Soul

“Please be a traveler, not a tourist. Try new things, meet new people, and look beyond what’s right in front of you. Those are the keys to understanding this amazing world we live in.” - Andrew Zimmern

Get a map and grab your keys and hit the road. Driving is one of the best ways to see this amazing country we call the United States of America. The journey is just as much a part of the trip as the destination. If you see something interesting stop and check it out. Get off the interstate and take the long way round you never know what you might find. Don't forget to bring some tools, food, water and some camping gear. Breakdowns happen, bad weather and traffic just embrace it all as part of the experience. Don't be in to much of a hurry, slow down and enjoy yourself. There is adventure out there now go find and find it. There are approximately 4,071,000 miles of road in the U.S. how many have you traveled?
