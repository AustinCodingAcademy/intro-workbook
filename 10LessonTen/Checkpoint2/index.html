A B O U T | U S

Board & video games have been a large part of my life since I was a child. It's a great medium storytelling, spontaneous creativity, and experimenting with different social roles. As I got older, I realized there was a really stupid stigma around gaming, especially tabletop. It's seen as something on the societal fringe: for losers, nerds, weirdos. I've never understood how getting sloshed at clubs with strangers tryin' to bone was somehow cooler than getting sloshed with your friends, making ridiculous shit up. So I started this website to inspire others to feel comfortable, and possibly even cool, indulging in their more authentic and playful sides.
