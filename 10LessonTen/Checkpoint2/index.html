Water Hole Required

On a hot summer days in Central Texas, we dogs really love cooling off in water.
For any park to achieve a top paw rating from us, water is required. Pet parents
should definitely bring floaty toys. My personal favorites are made by Chuckit. I like
Gator, Sharkey and especially Ducky. Also, you still need fresh water for your pet.
And remember,even though we love to swim, tasty treats are always much appreciated.
Once you get into longer hikes, you may want to bring food. Mid-size to large dogs
should be able to carry their own supplies with a doggie backpack.
