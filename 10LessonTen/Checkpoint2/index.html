Christian Based Education

Serving the city of Waxahachie and surrounding areas since 1982, we provide Christian based education for Preschool 2 - 4 year olds and Kindergarten through upper grades. We want our parents and children to look forward to an interesting and challenging experience at school. We look forward to watching your student grow intellectually, physically, and spiritually as they progress in becoming a mature adult who is a contributing member of society. We believe the well-being of American values can only be perpetuated through the training and upbringing of our young people. Thank you for entrusting your student to our care.

Parent Partners

The success of First Christian Day School relies heavily on the support and participation of our parents in fundraising, as well as serving other needs of the school. Through our program, Parents as Partners, families are encouraged to take part in a wide variety of volunteer opportunities that support FCDS. Parents as Partners involves all parents and teachers.

Support FCDS

First Christian Day School is a 501c3 non-profit organization. The success of our school relies heavily on our annual fundraising efforts and the support of our families, friends, community members and businesses. If you would like to make a tax-deductible contribution to support First Christian Day School, please contact us today.

STEM Scouts

Using experiential activities and interaction with STEM professionals, the goal of the STEM Scouts program is to help young people grow in character and skills as they explore their curiosity about STEM fields.
