Project Manager

I have spent the better part of the last 26 years managing projects for small to extremely large projects. The most significant role I was with the U. S. I was responsible for design review, submittal review and approval, and completing inspections in the field for compliance to codes, standards and the design of each low voltage system. The scope of construction included hospitals, instructional facilities, living quarters, administration facilities, dining facilities, parking garages, and renovation of historic buildings on Fort Sam Houston, Camp Bullis, Randolph Air Force Base and Lackland Air Force Base. At the height of construction I will be responsible for over 100 projects in various stages of construction.
