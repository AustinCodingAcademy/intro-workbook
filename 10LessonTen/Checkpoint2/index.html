Lhasa-mix, 7 years, male, 18 pounds

Bucky is a firm believer that you are only as old as you feel, and he feels (and acts) like he is half his age.

Buddy

Shih Tzu, 6 years, male, 11 pounds

Buddy is a sweet, sweet boy. He has a very easy going personality. He loves to cuddle close to your neck.

Shih Tzu/Poodle mix, 11 years, male, 12 pounds

Dustin came to us from AAC with a dislocated hip. Dustin just had a hip surgery and is doing well.
