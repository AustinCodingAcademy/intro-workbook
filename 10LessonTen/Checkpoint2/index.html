4's Co-ed Captain's Draft

Join us for Volleyball Austin inaugural summer sand league! Season goes for 8 weeks with 2 weeks of double elimination tournament play! Team are drafted by a captain based on either your rating from our indoor league or from one of our up coming rating sessions. Teams consists of at least 2 girls with a maximum of 5 people on a team.

Price

$40 a person or $140 for a team ($20 for a fifth). Late registration goes up $10 a person.

Swag

Team shirts included as well as two drink tickets for tournamnet play! Sunglasses provided!

Prizes

  • 1st place team get a $100 bar tab and 4 beach balls!
  • Second place gets a $50 bar tab!
  • Third place gets a high five!

Fun

Mid season tournamnet, serve accurancy contest, king of the beach and more fun events through the season!
