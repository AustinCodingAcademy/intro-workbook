THE ORIGINAL SK8-HI

Introduced in 1978 as Style #38

The newest addition to the SK8-HI line

Introduced in 1978, the SK8-Hi or Style #38 Lace-Up added an ankle high padded collar for extra protection to skaters. It was just the second shoe from Vans, after the Old Skool, to feature the sidestripe.

Samuria Warrior Era 59

The Samurai Warrior Era 59, a low top lace-up skate shoe, features sturdy double-stitched canvas and leather uppers with metal eyelets, interior print detailing, heel accents, padded collars for support and flexibility, and signature rubber waffle outsoles.

Twill Prison Issue

The Prison Issue, a retro silhouette originally known as Style #23, features twill uppers, an updated toecap, and resized straps for ultimate comfort and support. It also includes signature rubber waffle outsoles.

Suede Lampin

The Lampin, a retro 90s skate style evolved for a modern fit, features suede uppers, scaled sidewalls, metal eyelets, and signature rubber waffle outsoles.

S&L BRIGATA LITE

The S&L Brigata Lite has been reengineered using innovative construction methods to improve cushioning, increase flexibility, and reduce the overall weight. The Brigata Lite also includes enhanced heel comfort and UltraCush Lite outsoles for a long lasting, extremely lightweight feel.
