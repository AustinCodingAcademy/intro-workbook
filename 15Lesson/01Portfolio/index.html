A Little About Luis

I'm at the beginning stages of my web-development career. I was born in New Mexico and raised in Arizona. I love my Desert South-West upbringing. After High School I moved to Chicago with the dreams of becoming a actor. After 4 years at Columbia College I moved to Austin to help my family start a business. We have needed a website for Dolce Vita so I decided that I should get into web design and development to make this happen and with Austin Coding Academy and Capital Factory I am well on my way to becoming a full Full Stack Web Developer.
