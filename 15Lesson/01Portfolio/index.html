I am currently a graphic and web designer with Dos Mundos Creative.

In college I dual-majored in Illustration and Art History at Parsons School of Design/The New School. Then I worked for Martha Stewart where I was responsible for designing all kinds of holiday merchandise products. During my time there, I also oversaw part of a massive file-archiving project through which I found a love for databases and information management.

In my current job with Dos Mundos Creative, I’ve done branding and layout work for a variety of businesses in town, including the entire rebrand and repackaging of local açaí cafe Blenders & Bowls. I’ve also been doing a lot of Wordpress web development, through which I’ve found that my strengths and interests lie in U/X and Web Development.

Outside of work I’ve been taking courses with Austin Coding Academy for the last few months to enhance my coding abilities, and I’ve also been working with a friend/mentor to develop a static web-app on S3.
