MediCenter is Cape Girardeau and Jackson’s locally owned and operated pharmacy. We make a commitment to our customers to ensure you receive prescriptions as your healthcare provider prescribes; your prescriptions are ready in a timely manner, usually fifteen minutes or less if you choose to wait; and you are welcomed and treated with a smile you would expect from a friend. Our experienced pharmacist and staff welcome all questions, as our goal is to provide the support and advise so that your overall health and wellbeing is optimized in your daily life and actions. Expect more from a pharmacy when you choose MediCenter Pharmacy.
