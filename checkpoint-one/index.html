Welcome to Brown Sugar Bakery!

Why We Started

Brown Sugar Bakery is a lifelong dream that I've had since I was four years old. Stemming from my grandfather, I was born into a family of chefs. I remember being 12 and instead of planning on what I would get for Christmas, I would be planning dessert menus. I finally decided to turn my dream into reality in the spring of 2016.

What We're About

At BSB we pride ourselves on being a from-scratch bakery. We like to keep our carbon footprint as small as possible. Community means a lot to us so we keep our products as local as possible.
