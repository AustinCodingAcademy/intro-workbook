About

Hi. My name is Lisa DiRocco. I am a freelance illustrator in Austin, Texas. I love to illustrate all animals but especially ones with fangs!!! My favorite animals are dogs and sharks. I am currently showing my art in shows, selling prints, and painting pet portraits. I would love to one day illustrate children's books. I share my home with a cute dog who is a black lab named Bunny.
