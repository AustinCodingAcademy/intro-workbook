The Coffee

COFFEE: UNPLUGGED

Revisiting the craft of 19th century coffee roasters, we built a one-of-a-kind hearth, brick by brick, with no power cord or start button. It takes three people to custom hand-roast our coffee every day.

COFFEE BEANS

Our beans are the best of the best - Central and South America, Africa and Indonesia.
90% Fair Trade 100% Organic 100% Arabica
Zero Shelf Stock - All orders custom roasted and shipped.

TEXAS OAK

Our days begin with collecting, hand-splitting and stacking one of nature’s finest resources: Texas Oak.

FLAME

We tend the flames as the oak heats the firebrick to the perfect temperature. The difference between good coffee and great coffee is the control and consistency of the heat. Sight, sound and smell are the only tools employed to determine the readiness of both the fire we tend, and the coffee we roast.

RHYTHM

We use no gas or electricity in our commercial roaster. We employ a wood fire for heat and human ingenuity (muscle) to power our roaster- spinning the wheels at the perfect R.P.M.

PERFECTION

The wood, flames, firebrick and smoke do the heavy lifting, while we tend the fire and keep the beans in motion…The coffee tells US when it’s ready!

FAMILY

We drew upon our collective talents to build a roaster true to our heritage fired by our passion for great coffee. We strive to craft a coffee worthy of our efforts and a business that will keep us together for years to come.
