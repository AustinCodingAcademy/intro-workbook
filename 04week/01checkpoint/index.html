    Who's on your Grid?

    Grid is a location-based mobile application that allows you to connect with LinkedIn professionals around you. Grid provides you a list-view of professionals in your hyper-location whom your can meet face-to-face, expanding your network. Grid saves you time by showing you exactly who to connect with at your next professional conference. Often times its hard to know who to talk to and how to network with.

      Download Grid onto your smartphone and login using you LinkedIn profile.
      Turn on your location services and see other LinkedIn professionals who are nearby your current location.
      See a list of people who are in your current location and who are relevant to your industry.
      Scroll through the list-view to see which contacts are relevant. Message them directly and add them to your network.
