DAY FIVE: NICE TO MEET YOU, WILSON

The best volleyball I could ask for.

Well, it's been two days on this island. Today I stumbled across a packaged volleyball. I drew a face on it. I named it Wilson.

DAY FOUR: COLD, SCARED & LONELY

I can't help but wonder if I'll ever see another face again...

You wouldn't expect a beautiful island like this to be so cold at night.
I keep wondering if anybody is out there looking for me. I keep hoping.

DAY THREE: CAUGHT A FISH TODAY

I've never felt more primal. I wonder if anybody knows I'm even missing.

I've found that I act well under pressure. I have more raw instinct than I knew of.
Today, I went out and caught a fish with my bare hands. I'll never forget this day.

DAY TWO: FINDING FOOD

I'm real hungry. Maybe I could catch a fish...here goes nothing

It's only day two and I've been craving funions and coco-cola. I wonder if I can find some sort of tropical fish on the shoreline.

DAY ONE: CRASHED!

I think I'm the only survivor.

I went off to deliever these packages...the next moment we had crashed. I think I'm a lone survivor. Hopefully help comes soon.
