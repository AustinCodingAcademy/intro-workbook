MY NEIGHBOR WILLIE

July 21, 2017

Shotgun Willie! Roll me up and smoke me when I die. Whiskey river take my mind. Luck ranch is in Spicewood, Texas. Willie's Cutt and Putt golf course where the motto is "Bring Your Own Grass". There's a double meaning to that statement. Teh Cutt and Putt is in Briarcliff where I live on the sixth hole. You where always on my mind. I'm crazy,crazy for feeling so lonely I'm crazy, crazy for feeling so blue. Will the circle be unbrowken. I'll fly away. Angel flying to close to the ground. Lot's of green stuff.
