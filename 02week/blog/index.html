Biblical Books Blog

What's This Blog About?

For my whole adult life I have been interested in the study of the Bible. I am always on the lookout for books that can give me insight into its gems of wisdom and understanding. Nothing can replace reading the Word God itself, having said that, there is great benefit to reading the books of men who have dedicated their time and effort, even their lives to searching out the hidden treasure in the Bible.
I started this blog for other truth-seakers who are endeavouring to glean from the lessons that the bible has to offer in hopes that it will be usefull and beneficial in finding great books to read.
If you have any questions about the books or the bible in general, feel free toemailme. I may able to help direct you to a great resource.

Justification: God's Plan, Paul's Vision by N.T. Wright

This is the most exiting book I have ever read on the topic of Justification. It is was hard to set down once I started to read it. N.T. Wright expertly shows that Justification has its roots in the Old Testament and that it is just one part of a larger whole of what makes up salvation in the Bible. More specifically it is about the covenant that YHWH made with Abraham. It is a must read for all those about to journey on the road of justification.

Rating: Great

The Art of Reading Scripture by Ellen Davis and Richard Hays

Many times when the thought of Biblical study arises, one is automatically thrust into a technical endeavor. This book proved to me over and over that the artistic side of Biblical exegesis is just as vital to the understanding of God's Word as the technical. Highly recomended!

Rating: Great

Paul and The Faithfulness of God by N.T. Wright

To say that this book is a game-changer would be an understatement! He integrates historical, literary, philosophical and theological approaches to the Bible superbly. He explores Pauls extraordinary achievement of unveiling Judaism around a crucified and risen Messiah. Each chapter reveals something profound about the faithfulness of God freshly revealed in our Messiah and conveyed to us by the Spirit. This is a must read!

Rating: Great
