San Antonio Artists to Watch

Exceptional local photographers, musicians, graphic designers, stylists and interdisciplinary artists.

Interdisciplinary Artist
Daniela Riojas

Daniela Riojas is an interdisciplinary artist from the U.S. – Mexico, border based out of San Antonio, TX; specializing in performance, photography, film, installation, and music. Her work deals in issues concerning reconnection to indigeny, ancient ritual, decolonization, rediscovery of the matriarch, and investigation of the self. Daniela is the lead singer and composer for progressive, fusion band, Femina-X.

Online Fashion Personality
Burgundy Woods

Burgundy Woods created Style Lush TV, San Antonio’s online fashion and style network and the San Antonio Fashion Awards (SAFA). SAFA is a community driven awards ceremony that celebrates the entire fashion community and acknowledges those individuals in San Antonio who help push the local fashion and style industry forward.

Choreographer
Stephan Gaeth

Stephan Gaeth is a modern dancer and San Antonio Artist Foundation Award Recipient who has produced and performed in several dance theater productions. He is the creator and facilitator of Improv Jam and Contact Lab, a monthly artist jam open to all. He teaches weekly dance classes and is a Board Member of the San Antonio Dance Umbrella.
