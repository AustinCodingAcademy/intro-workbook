Predestination, what comes first?

July 22, 2017

Ethan Hawke, plays a man *wink* who makes multiple jumps to different time periods to hopefully prevent a bomber from killing thousands of people in New York City.. the history of the characters is introduced in a retelling a la "Forest Gump" but about thirty minutes in, the story telling style changes and the plot makes some jaw dropping twists. It trumps the other paradoxial time travelling golden hen, Time Crimes in the production value and acting departments. Even if it can't shake off the convoluted specifics that accompany a time travelling plot, I would recommend fans of the genre to take a look at their schedule and make some time to watch it.
