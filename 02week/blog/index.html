Top 5 Choices for Study Spots

Posted: April 20th 2017

I've decided to share with you my top 5 places that I like to go and study and/or read! All of which offer Wi-Fi because why would I list a place that didn't offer it!?

  • Austin Hookah Lounge

    • Enjoy some hookah, coffee, or you can even BYOB if you like! Also, they allow outside food!

  • Bennu Coffee

    • A place that offers great coffee, pizza by the slice, and it's 24 hours! Good luck finding parking.

  • Epoch Coffee

    • Lots of seating here, very hip environment with great music on rotation. This place is also 24 hours.

  • Sa-Ten

    • This cute, asian-inspired place is located in the so-called "Gentrification" area of East Austin. Offers great food, great coffee, and even greater homemade lemonade. Here you can take a break and wander around at the art galleries and stores that neighbor this place.

  • Summer Moon Coffee

    • In my opinion, this place has the best latte's hands down with their famous 'Moon Milk'.
