OSITA ANUSI II

A leader amongst the his peer's in music near and far, Osita Anusi II brought his critical thinking skills gained from his formative years as a biochemistry major at UTSA, to the Saint Mary's strip with his formulaic approach to having a good time paired with great tunes

Hailing from Houston, Texas Osita spent a majority of his high school career in service to the community, spending years volunteering with the YMCA and American Red Cross.

In 2009 Osita moved to San Antonio to pursue a degree in biochemistry and almost immediately he immersed himself in the San Antonio music scene starting several bands the most notable one being "The Bolos".

Out of popular demand and necessity, Osita started Fuzzland Productions with fellow musician Nick Ivarra. With Their combined years of touring experience they were able to use connections they made to bring fresh music from all over the world. Within a short period of time Osita found himself heading a yearly music festival called Hangover Fest

Currently Osita has been spending time working on the future of Fuzzland Productions ands its growth while also working on his personal growth and future by learning new coding languages that will be integral in taking the next steps towards changing the way live music and technology will coexist with one another
