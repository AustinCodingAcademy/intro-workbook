Ramona Neafie Biography

Ramona Neafie is a professionally trained librarian who reluctantly fell in with this digital fad when they took away her card catalog and AACR2 reference manual.

She found that fierce research skills and well-honed information organization habits were not entirely wasted online. As she's explored the various corners of the digitial information world, she's worked with search engines, accessibility, usability, analytics, and programming.

Her library is cataloged online using Library Thing. A few of the books may be her husbands'.

She enjoys loose tea, cooperative board games, reading obscure history, and debating ethics with her younger cat. The older cat is a total lightweight.
