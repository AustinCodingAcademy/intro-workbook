Shay Hoffman: Abbreviated Bio

Shay grew up in the Rio Grande Valley, about ten miles from the border and forty miles from Brownsville. She graduated from high school in the top 10% of her class, which afforded her the opportunity to attend the University of Texas at Austin. She earned a Liberal Arts degree, completing her dual majors, English Literature and Geography with an emphasis in Sustainability and Geographic information systems. Shay graduated with college honors in the spring of 2017, and spent three consecutive summers working as a sportswriter for the publication, The Daily Texan.

Upon graduation, Shay enrolled in Austin Coding Academy to learn about web development, add to her idenity capital and broaden her range of employable skills. Upon completion of the nine month course, she plans to either look for work as a junior web developer, or use her new skillset to aid in her postgraduate work as she pursues either a Juris Doctor or a Masters in Business Administration.
