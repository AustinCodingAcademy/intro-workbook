Kristina Robinson Bio


Although not a native Texan, Kristina has lived in the Lone Star state for most of her life and considers herself a Texan. She resides in San Antonio, TX, with her husband and two boys, and a variety of foster animals at any given time. Her pastimes include reading, knitting, paper crafting, painting, and playing video games with (and without) her sons.
