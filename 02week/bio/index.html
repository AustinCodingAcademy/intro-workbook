ABOUT ME

Christy hails from the midwest in all it's charm from the Chicago area. She grew a fascination with art and design at a young age, and has pursued many creative areas since then. From film to fashion to digital design she has used multiple creative outlets to form a wellrounded knowledge of all elements and forms. She is going on 5 years of experience in a professional Creative setting spanning film and tv, design, art direction, and social media consulting. She loves to attend concerts and film festivals in her spare time and volunteer in local charity benefits and shelters.
