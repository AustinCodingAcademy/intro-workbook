    Hello



    My name is Veronica Cardenas, and I enjoy working with computers to create designs. My favorite programs are Photoshop, Illustrator, and Coda. I enjoy taking photographs, but the real fun starts when it comes to the editing part. I can get a lot more creative and reach out of my comfort zone. Some background information about me is that I am Hispanic. I grew up most of my life in Brownsville, Texas and spent most of my days at the beach. I like the outdoors and summer days. I also have a deep appreciation and love for the ocean. My favorite projects consist of anything that has to do with the beach, surfing, long boarding and skating. I enjoy doing projects dedicated for others because it gives more excitement and purpose to my designs.
