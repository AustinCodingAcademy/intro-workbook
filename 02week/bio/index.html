About Brady

Brady Porche is a staff reporter at CreditCards.com. He has 15 years' experience as a journalist, copywriter and digital media specialist. As a trade journalist, Porche has covered a wide variety of industries, including oil and gas, water and wastewater, travel and leisure, professional sports and more. He now writes regular news updates and feature stories related to personal finance, credit scoring, credit reports and consumer debt.

A native of Houma, Louisiana, Porche has a bachelor's degree in mass communication from LSU.
