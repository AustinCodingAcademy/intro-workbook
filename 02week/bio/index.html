Ramona Neafie Biography

Ramona Neafie is a professional trained librarian who reluctantly fell in with this digital fad when they took away her card catalog and MARCive reference manual.

She found that fierce research skills and well-honed information organization habits were not entirely wasted online.

Her library is cataloged online using Library Thing. A few of the books may be her husbands'.

She enjoys loose tea, cooperative board games, reading obscure history, and debating ethics with her younger cat. The older cat is a total lightweight.
