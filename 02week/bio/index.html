My Bio

Things were fine in my early life, I thought. I was born in 1971 in a small town in Mexico. I lived in Mexico til about 10 years old and that's when my parents had decided to move to California. One of my first impressions that I have of the U.S. is of all the different types of neon signs lit up at night in the neighborhood convenience stores. We had moved to San Jose, California, a beautiful city. One of my older brothers was very big on going to college and so college became one of my goals. My older sister also went on to college and became an elementary teacher. At that time a teaching career was not my favorite career, but I never looked down at it. As we got older we decided to move down to Texas. And so we moved to another small town called Seguin. As the years went by I moved to San Marcos and Austin. I finished my college at Texas State University.
