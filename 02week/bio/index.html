Originally born in Washington, Jeffrey has lived on a number of military installations throughout the U.S. and as a child spent the most time in Seguin, Texas becoming a lifelong comic book reader. After graduating high school, he joined the U.S. Army where he served over 20 years in both active and reserve military units as well as participated in a number of deployments in numerous countries (most notably Iraq). After serving our country for over 20 years, Jeffrey has held a number of IT jobs within the government, healthcare, financial and private sectors. He currently resides in Converse, Texas with his wife and two chlidren. His hobbies include writing and drawing with the hope of creating a videogame or comic book series, playing videogames and spending time with his family.
