Hello World! I'm Gwen Spretell.

Professional History


Gwen is an accomplished software executive with over 20 years experience in the software industry. Gwen is currently President and CEO of Liquid Engines, a corporate tax management and optimization software company. Her previous executive positions include president and CEO of Intelligent Results, Inc. and COO of BackWeb Technologies, Inc. Before joining BackWeb, Ms. Spertell spent eight years at Oracle Corporation, where she served in various management positions for sales, consulting, and alliance development, with her most recent position serving as vice president of global ISV alliances. She was previously president and CEO of Pacific Western Information Systems, where she served as the key architect for its flagship product, CompuTrust, a trust accounting application. Ms. Spertell earned her BA in economics from Rutgers University.
