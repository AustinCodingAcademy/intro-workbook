About Me

I'm from Houston, Texas. I've lived here all my life. From a young age I was very athletic, playing baseball, football, soccer, and basketball since age 6. I rode this athleticism as far as I could, resulting in a college football career. In the end, though, I got hurt one too many times to keep playing football so I transferred to the University of Oklahoma. At OU, I am a double major and am pretty involved on campus. I joined a fraternity, ran for officer positions, and participated in a lot of fundraising drives both for local initiatives as well as things close to home, like the aftermath of Hurricane Harvey. One thing I'm particularly proud of is my fundraising effort for Harvey; we raised nearly $5,000 for the Houston Food Bank and paid for over 10,000 meals.
