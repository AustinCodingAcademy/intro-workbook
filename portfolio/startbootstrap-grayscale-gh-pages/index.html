¿Quién soy yo?

I'm a UX Researcher at JUST Community - a Micro-Finance organization in Austin, TX striving to change the conversation around low-income entrepreneurs. The clients we serve are hard-working Latinas who have a survival instinct like no other - one I recognize within the Latinas in my own family.

With the use of technology and human-centered design, I believe that we can create a powerful Community that facilitates less stressed and more joyful lives.

With this mission in mind, I enrolled into the Austin Coding Academy this past July and will be graduating next April with the knowledge of a Full Stack Developer.
