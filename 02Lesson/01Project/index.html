Run Asturias!

Running trips in the Mountains of Northern Spain

With Run Asturias, you will complete runs in the mountains in the northern part of Asturias. We are based out of an area called "El Sueve", which sits at the bottom of the mountain range, along the sea line. Our runs begin from the bottom of the Picos de Europa mountain range and continue a climb of 50km. Runners stay in quaint lodgings at the base of the mountain range, 5 minutes walk from the beach.

What's Included

Our trips include the following:

  • Accomadations for 5 nights in rural apartments
  • Breakfast and lunch - dinner on your own
  • 3 runs, morning and evening, depending on day
  • Tour of El Sueve

Why Asturias?

Asturias is rich with history. It offers excellent trail running through woods and mountain ranges, with views of the ocean from high above. It's cuisine, from bean stews, roasted meats to local ciders, is one of the best in the world. The summer climate is perfect for long or short runs, with cooler temperatures than in the rest of the penisula. We are certain that you will enjoy your time there and will want to return!
