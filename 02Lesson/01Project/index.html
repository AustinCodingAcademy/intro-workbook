About

What can we say Sailor Pluto is our hero and we love coffee! We started in 1999 with two people who just loved coffee and had a love of the planet pluto. We have a wonderful full list of coffee with celestial themes and decorate each drink with celestial latte art for your pleasure. Pluto Coffee is also home to Pluto milk, a top secret recipe. Come try our coffee and experience Pluto Coffee House!
