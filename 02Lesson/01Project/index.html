Call Roy's Motorcyle Taxidermy! With years of experience of taxidermy of large game animals, mixed with a passion for handbuilt motorcycles, Roy has invented a unique process to turn that bull into a HOG!! Check out all the animals he can transform into something faster than it has ever been:

  • Deer
  • Cows
  • Boar (for 150cc or smaller)
  • Buffalo
  • Giraffe (for choppers)

What's Roy's process?

  1. First, you kill an animal. Make sure it's got a ribcage large enough for the engine you'd like.
  2. Bring Roy the animal, and choose and engine and style of motorcycle.
  3. Roy strips the bones and builds the bike, inserting the animal as it most ergonomically fits.
  4. Ride into the sunset on your conquered beast!
