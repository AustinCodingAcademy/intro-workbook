Hillary Clinton's Breaking Tweet: "Donald Trump Is Not A Nice Guy"

On Thursday night, Hillary Clinton released a tweet on her official Twitter account describing her distaste for Mr. Donald Trump. In the tweet she described how much of America feels about Donald Trump right now in just a few short, but inspiring words.

We asked Wilson, a 50 year old republican voter what he thought about her controversial tweet. "Well, I think that Donald Trump is nice, he's very nice. In fact, I think that Hillary Clinton is even less nice than Donald Trump is." After sharing his opinion on the matter Wilson climbed into his Tesla X and drove back to his Los Angeles mansion.
