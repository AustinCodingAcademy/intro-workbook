HEB Buys Twin Oak Center at Oltorf, Congress

In a significant South Austin real estate deal, grocery chain H-E-B has purchased the Twin Oaks Shopping Center, a decades-old, 10-acre property at South Congress Avenue and Oltorf Street — catty-corner across Congress from one of H-E-B’s oldest and most popular Austin stores.

Austin, Tx Economy

The city is home to development centers and headquarters for many technology corporations, adopting the nickname Silicon Hills in the 1990s. Recently, however, the current official slogan promotes Austin as The Live Music Capital of the World, a reference to the many musicians and live music venues within the area. One major event, South by Southwest, is one of the largest music festivals in the U.S.
