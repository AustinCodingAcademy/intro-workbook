Patriots fans buying season's most expensive tickets for Tom Brady's return

Tom Brady is back, and New England Patriots fans are paying a premium to see his first home game of the 2016 season. Sunday’s contest at Gillette Stadium against the Cincinnati Bengals shapes up as the most in-demand and, in turn, pricey game so far in this NFL season. The average price on the secondary ticket market is about $550 as of Friday, a price tag that exceeds last January’s divisional playoff game against the Kansas City Chiefs.
