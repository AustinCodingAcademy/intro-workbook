It's All About Me

Hello! My name is Collin Davis. I am a 29 year old small town boy, originally from Kerrville, Texas. I moved to Austin in 2003 and began my culinary adventures through this wild city. From Lake Austin and North Austin, down to the far reaches of South Austin, I have lived and worked in every part of the city. In 2008 I met the love of my life Stephanie, and in 2011, we started our journey into parenthood with our daughter Averi. By 2014 we were ready for round two and welcomed our son Jasper into this world. A year later we signed a mortgage on the southeast end of town, and became bonafide home owners! Now that we are somewhat settled, it's time to try my hand at something new. Coding here I come!!!
