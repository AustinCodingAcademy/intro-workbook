About

For over 25 years, I have been involved in many aspects of creative arts. I am a musician who loves to dabble in digital media and I'm currently in training to become a UX/UI front-end web developer.

I am an artist at heart who strives to communicate truth with excellence in all that I do. My hope is that through my talents and service, I can help others live a better life.
