Confirming their domination in Kata, Spain ended up in the finals of Male and Female Team Kata competitions. The squad formed by Damian Quintero, Jose Manuel Carbonell and Francisco Jose Salazar reached the Male Team Kata final after beating Russia in the quarterfinal and Croatia in the semifinal, who will be fighting for the bronze.

On the other side of the pool, the kata team of France consisted of Enzo Montarello, Lucas Jeannot and Ahmed Zemouri made it to the finals by beating the teams of Austria and Turkey, who will also be fighting for the 2nd bronze medal.
