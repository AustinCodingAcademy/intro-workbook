Welcome to MoToATX!

MoToATX is a Texas Track day motorcycle rider's school, which provides instructional assistance for a wide variety of skill levels. From the street rider up to the most experienced licensed racer and everything in between, our focus is to bring you the most comprehensive set of tools available. We aim to help you increase your riding proficiency and safety.

You can find one of our many Texas track day events at the following race tracks: Texas World Speedway (TWS), MSR-Houston (MSRC), MSR at Cresson (MSRC), Circuit of The Americas (COTA) and Eagles Canyon Raceway (ECR).

Fuel can be purchased onsite. Camping is allowed; check with each track for onsite or outside campgrounds. Parking is allowed in designated spaces only. Vehicles & trailers are not allowed under any of the covered areas during the event.

Additional Info:

MoToATX is a school - we are not a racing organization; we do not hold races or competitions. You ride under the guidance of an instructor at your own pace.
