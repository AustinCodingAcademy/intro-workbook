What is The Meal Fiasco?

The Meal Fiasco (or TMF) is an easy, affordable meal preparation service designed to meet the needs of people on the go.

Whether you don't enjoy cooking or simply don't have the time, The Meal Fiasco has a plan for you! Deliveries are on Sundays (unless otherwise requested), and you will receive a text beforehand with an estimated delivery time. There is a $5 delivery fee due at the time of drop off.
