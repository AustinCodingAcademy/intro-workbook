Rocky Mountain High Adventure Tours

Welcome to Rocky Mountain High Adventure Tours, Denver's premier tour bus service to Mount Evans. Our tours leave from the City's transit hub, Union Station and transport you to the top of the world!


The Mount Evans Scenic Byway is one of the most beautiful drives in North America, it's only a couple of hours from central Denver to the Summit. The byway is 28 miles (45 km) in length and gains over 7,000 feet (2,100 m) of elevation. Achieving a final elevation of 14,130 feet (4,310 m), this is the highest paved road in North America.

Our luxury coaches conveniently pick you up at Union Station. Sit back and relax as you climb through the wooded foothills. Next we have a stop in the quaint mountain town of Evergreen, for some shopping and refreshments. As the tour continues you'll pass beautiful forests, mountain lakes and alpine meadows.

There is abundant wildlife in the area including mountain goats, mountain sheep, elk, moose and black bears among hundreds of other species. As you continue up the hairpin turns you can see all of Denver spread below you. Finally, you reach the summit with a gorgeous view of the Rocky Mountains. You can climb the last few hundred feet to the very top and truly have a Rocky Mountain High Adventure!
