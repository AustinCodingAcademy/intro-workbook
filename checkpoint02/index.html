about me...

I'm a man on a quest to see everything from every angle. I question my assumptions; I break them apart and open them up. Knowing how and why is a vital part of knowing what something is.

There are so many worthwhile things to care about in the world, and I wish I had a lifetime for each of them. I'm an environmentalist, a space junky, a friend to animals, a gardener, and a game enthusiast.

I see creativity as integral to the human experience. Without subjective perception we're just walking supercomputers. And those experiences, conscious and subconscious, are expressed through creative projects. Writing and manipulating code is a way to imprint our humanity onto machines.

I believe all things are beautiful from the right distance. Finding that distance is a lifelong pursuit. Sharing the beauty we find is a lifelong passion.
